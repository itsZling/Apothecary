Anime

My earliest memory of anime is watching a random episode of Naruto at my friend's house during a sleepover. I was probably only in 7th grade, but it seemed like the coolest thing in the world at the time. With no sports or after-school activities at the time, I found myself doing anything that appeared in my recommendations. Eventually, I started tracking all the series I watched and now have over 24,000 hours watched total across 300 different series.

Manga

I started collecting manga around April 2022, with my first series being Beastars after finishing the second season. Since then, I've collected over 300 volumes and read over 4,500 chapters.

I enjoy returning to my room with some new volumes and reorganizing my bookshelf with the latest editions.
